Bonito
Snorkeling in Bonito

Bonito is a city nestled on the Pantanal region of Mato Grosso do Sul, Brazil. The city is of little note, but the surrounding region is known for spectacular clearwater river snorkeling, overall natural beauty and lush fauna & flora, and it is often regarded as a model case of sustainable tourism in Brazil. The river waters of Bonito are crystal clear, often regarded as natural aquariums.
